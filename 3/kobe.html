About KOBE BRYANT

Kobe Bryant,who was born in California(1978-2020),used to be NBA legendary basketball player.Kobe was selected by Los Angeles Lakers in rank 13th,and has a very successful 20-year career.

Kobe retired in 2016,April 14th.Unfortunately,Kobe was dead in an air accident at the age of 41.World lost a fantastic basktball player.
